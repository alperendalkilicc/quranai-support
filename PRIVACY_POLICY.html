Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful

We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with Islamic principles of trust (Amanah) and respect for personal boundaries.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Quran AI ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to transparency and protecting your privacy rights.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your email address and chosen username
  • Profile Preferences: Your selected language, reading habits, and spiritual preferences
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our app, including reading progress and feature usage

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Analytics Data: App performance metrics and crash reports (anonymized)
  • Location Data: Only if you explicitly grant permission for prayer time features

Islamic Commitment: We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide you with a meaningful spiritual experience. We respect the Islamic principle of privacy (Sitr).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized Quranic verse recommendations
  • Track your spiritual reading progress
  • Improve our AI algorithms for better Islamic content
  • Send you relevant spiritual reminders (if opted in)
  • Provide customer support
  • Process subscription payments

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Important: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your spiritual journey is private and sacred.

We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate our app (e.g., cloud storage, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (with continued privacy protection)

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only
  • Secure payment processing through trusted providers

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for users of all ages, including children learning about Islam. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking technologies:

  • Essential cookies for app functionality
  • Analytics cookies to improve user experience (anonymized)
  • No advertising or behavioral tracking cookies

10. Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered content generation (no personal data shared)
  • Firebase: For app analytics and performance monitoring

11. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You can request deletion of your account at any time.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by email.

13. Islamic Values and Privacy

Our approach to privacy is guided by Islamic principles:

  • Amanah (Trust): We consider your data a trust and handle it with care
  • Sitr (Privacy): We respect your right to privacy and confidentiality
  • Adl (Justice): We are transparent and fair in our data practices
  • Ihsan (Excellence): We strive for the highest standards in data protection
