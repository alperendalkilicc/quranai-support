Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage your subscription through your device's settings:

  • iOS: Settings > App Store > Subscriptions
  • Android: Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions

How is my data protected?

We take your privacy seriously. All your data is stored locally on your device and encrypted. We only collect minimal data necessary for app functionality.

How can I customize notifications?

Go to Settings > Notifications in the app to customize your notification preferences, including frequency and types of notifications.

How does the widget work?

The QuranAI widget brings daily inspirational verses to your home screen. To add the widget:

  • Long press on an empty area of your home screen
  • Tap "Add Widget"
  • Find QuranAI and select your preferred widget size

What are the benefits of premium membership?

Premium members get access to:

  • Unlimited verse archive
  • Advanced search filters
  • Custom commentary notes
  • Ad-free experience
  • Multi-language support
  • Advanced widget options

The app won't open or I'm experiencing issues

In this case, try the following steps:

  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates in the App Store
  • If the problem persists, contact us at the email address above

Which iOS versions are supported?

QuranAI supports iOS 15.0 and later versions. We recommend using the latest iOS version for the best experience.
